> Visa

A citizen of a foreign country who seeks to enter the Türkiye generally must first obtain a visa, which is placed in the traveler’s passport. The type of visa you must obtain is defined by Türkiye immigration law, and relates to the purpose of your travel. Please visit the official page here to find out what visa type is appropriate for you.

> Plug Types (Electricity)

Power plugs in Türkiye is European type. Participants from USA, UK, etc. may need a proper converter to use wall plugs in the hotel and/or conference halls.
