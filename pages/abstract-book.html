Abstract Book (EMR 2023)

We are excited to announce that the abstract book for EMR2023 is now available for download. Featuring research abstracts from 32 invited speakers, as well as 43 oral, 15 poster, and 5 Young Statistician Showcase presentations, the book offers a comprehensive look at the breadth and depth of the conference's research. Furthermore, a roundtable session featuring five speakers provided an opportunity for in-depth discussion and collaboration on key issues in the field. Finally, in addition to these presentations, EMR2023 also hosted three courses on a range of topics, further expanding attendees' knowledge base.

The abstract book is an essential resource for attendees looking to make the most of their conference experience. By previewing the research abstracts, you can identify the sessions and presentations that are most relevant to your interests and goals.

We invite you to download the abstract book from our conference website and explore the exciting research presented at EMR2023. Thank you for your participation, and we look forward to seeing you at the conference.
